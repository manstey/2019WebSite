PRE APPLICATION ADVICE - MAY 2019

What went wrong

This document was not placed on the Council website and was only found after a recent problematical Freedom of Information investigation.

This document was not mentioned in subsequent examination of the Planning Application and a number of the problems and the implicated local residents were not considered or consulted.

How things could be improved

All related Planning Documents should be placed onto the Council website so local residents are aware of the progress of an Application and any matters where they could be implicated.

Implicated local residents should be made aware and should be allowed to be consulted about identified problems
